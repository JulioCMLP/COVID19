How to protect yourself from the coronavirus?

5 gestures to slow progress

  • Do not touch your face: Generally and             especially during an epidemic, avoid as much as possible             bring your hands to your nose, your mouth or even your eyes.             The hands may have been in contact with surfaces             contaminated.
  • Avoid crowded places: It is recommended to avoid             very busy places and             gatherings, which are now prohibited if they exceed 100 people.             Hospitals are also to be avoided as much as possible for reasons             obvious. If you are forced to go there, scrupulously respect             hygiene measures.

  • Maintain a social distance: A safety distance of one meter must be observed             between yourself and a person who coughs or sneezes. It is the distance that allows             not be affected by droplets from the nose or mouth that may contain the             virus "insists our interlocutor.
